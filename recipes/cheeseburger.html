Cheese Burger Recipe

Description

A classic American sandwich featuring a juicy grilled beef patty topped with a slice of melted cheese, typically served on a toasted bun. Often layered with fresh lettuce, tomato, pickles, onions, and condiments like ketchup, mustard, or mayo, it strikes the perfect balance of savory, salty, and tangy flavors. Crispy on the outside and tender on the inside, the cheeseburger is a timeless comfort food favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs of ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon sweet relish
  • 1 tablespoon yellow mustard
  • 1 small grated white onion
  • 1 teaspoon black peeper
  • Cheese of your choice
  • Buns of your choice
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Sliced Onion

Steps

  1. Step 1: Add ground beef to bowl and break up meat
  2. Step 2: Sprinkle seasonings across meat and add relish, mustard, and grated onion
  3. Step 3: Get skillet hot with oil and place patties
  4. Step 4: Cook each patty for six minutes on each side
  5. Step 4: Add cheese and toppings
